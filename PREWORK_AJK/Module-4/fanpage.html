My Top Ten Muse Songs

Bio

The members of Muse, Matthew Bellamy (lead singer/guitarist), Dominic Howard (bass), and Chris Wolstenholme (drums) has been making music together since the early 1990's. Their discography has covered a spectrum of genres including Alt Rock, Punk/Blues, and and Space Rock among others. Muse has earned the Grammy Award's Best Rock Album in 2011 and 2016 as well as the UK Festival Awards Anthem of the Year in 2010 among many other awards and nominations.
